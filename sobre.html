Projeto

In the most depressed area of ​​the city of Porto, Campanhã, two galleries were born that have photography as their main protagonist and occupy two of the eleven warehouses on Rua de Miraflor. Most of the warehouses that had been abandoned for decades seemed doomed to collapse. It was its size (about 200 square meters), its height, the granite walls and the majestic doors that led photographers Manuela Matos Monteiro and João Lafuente to acquire two warehouses that are now two galleries: Espaço MIRA and MIRA FORUM.

One of the concerns was to recover the two places respecting their design and original materials, which are now considered examples of a successful rehabilitation. The initiative of the two photographers contaminated other wills and, at the moment, the eleven warehouses are mostly occupied by people related to the arts.

Espaço Mira

A gallery fundamentally linked to photography and its relationship with other arts and knowledge. It is with the artistic direction of José Maia that an exhibition program is being developed in which an «thinking the expanded field of photography», that is, to reflect photography in its multiple aspects. Conferences, film and performance cycles and exhibitions of artists’ books help to create a critical reflection on the place and role of photography inscribed in the city and in today’s world.

Artist residencies are also an important component of MIRA’s intervention. The gallery searches to always establish close relationships with the community and its institutions. The diversity of proposals and artistic expressions, the presence of works by artists from different generations has been one of the characteristics of Espaço MIRA’s intervention.

MIRA FORUM

MIRA FORUM it is the second warehouse on Rua de Miraflor, number 155. It is an interdisciplinary place that, due to the great diversity on offer, has been assuming the body of a cultural center in Campanhã.

Exhibitions, presentations and book launches, conferences, debates and gatherings, photographic tours and evenings, concerts, theater plays or dance shows are some of the activities that highlight the gallery.
